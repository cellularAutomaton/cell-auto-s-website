About

As a composer, I'm influenced by video game soundtracks and ambient and experimental music. I'm particularly interested in xenharmony, a branch of music that explores scales and intervals outside of Western norms. One of the hallmarks of my work is "xenmodality", where I merge modal harmonic styles with xenharmonic systems. My compositions often result from in-depth exploration of specific tunings.
